## Selected work

### 1) FAO Office of Innovation — ATIO Knowledge Base
- Analyze user behavior and knowledge-discovery flows across **5,744** agricultural technology and innovation records
- Evaluate and refine AI-enrichment prompts used to classify records
- Collaborate with UX, Drupal, taxonomy, and data-harvesting workstreams

### 2) Knowledge Graph + RAG Research
- Built knowledge graph-enhanced RAG architectures in **PyTorch** and **LangChain**
- Ran ablation studies on retrieval accuracy, latency, and context coherence
- Focused on **multi-hop question answering**

### 3) Cultural-Heritage Damage Inspection
- Led architecture and implementation of a **DETR-based** cultural-heritage damage-inspection system
- Achieved **F1 = 0.89**
- Resulted in **operational use**, a **software copyright**, and a **patent filing**

### 4) WaggleNet
- Contributed to the design, field deployment, and evaluation of a **LoRa-MQTT** beehive-monitoring system
- Achieved **100% packet delivery at 110 m**, **3.8 s mean latency**, and **95%+ uptime**
- Co-authored the resulting **arXiv preprint**
